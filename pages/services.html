Strategic consulting tailored to your business

I work with founder-led brands and fast-growing teams across food, retail, DTC, and consumer categories. Whether you need a thought partner on strategy or hands-on execution support, I'm here to help.

Core Services

Product Strategy & Vision

Bring clarity to your product direction. I help you understand your consumers, competitive landscape, and market opportunities—then translate that into a clear, executable product strategy.

  • Consumer insights and trend analysis
  • Product positioning and competitive differentiation
  • Concept development and validation
  • Assortment planning and portfolio optimization
  • Category expansion roadmapping
  • Pricing strategy and value positioning

Outcome: A clear product vision with market validation and a roadmap to execution.

Go-to-Market Planning & Launch Execution

Successful launches require cross-functional alignment and flawless execution. I work with your team to build a comprehensive GTM strategy and shepherd products to market with confidence.

  • GTM strategy and market entry planning
  • Launch readiness assessments
  • Cross-functional project management
  • Channel strategy and retail partnerships
  • Merchandising and promotional planning
  • Launch analytics and performance tracking

Outcome: Successful product launches with measurable impact on sales and market presence.

Operations & Supply Chain Optimization

Strong operations enable strategy execution. I help you optimize vendor partnerships, reduce costs, streamline lead times, and build the operational foundations for sustainable growth.

  • Vendor sourcing and partnership strategy
  • Cost negotiations and optimization
  • Lead time reduction and timeline management
  • Manufacturing and quality specifications
  • Inventory and supply chain management
  • Process streamlining and efficiency audits

Outcome: Efficient, scalable operations that support your growth without compromising quality.

Category Management & Growth

Grow profitable categories through data-driven insights and strategic execution. I bring experience managing categories across multiple retailers and direct-to-consumer channels.

  • Category performance analysis and insights
  • Trend forecasting and strategic foresight
  • Merchandising strategy and mix optimization
  • Promotional planning and trade strategy
  • SKU rationalization and assortment decisions
  • Competitive response and market dynamics

Outcome: Growing, profitable categories with strong market positions and customer preference.

How I Work

Project-Based Engagements

3-6 month focused projects. Ideal for specific initiatives like product launches, category expansion, or operational optimization.

Best for: Startups and teams with defined scope and timeline.

Ongoing Retainers

Regular advisory support, typically 2-4 days per month. Perfect for ongoing strategic guidance and execution support.

Best for: Growing companies that need consistent strategic input.

Full-Time Roles

Fractional COO, Chief Product Officer, or VP Strategy. Lead teams and drive strategic initiatives as part of your leadership team.

Best for: Companies in rapid growth or transition phases.

Advisory Board

Strategic counsel and accountability. Quarterly meetings to guide strategy and help navigate challenges.

Best for: Founders and CEOs seeking strategic sounding boards.
