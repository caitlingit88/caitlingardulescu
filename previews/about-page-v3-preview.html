About
Personal Side

Caitlin Gardulescu

I’m deeply energized by people, creativity, travel, and great food.

I'm from Ann Arbor, Michigan, and the places I've lived have shaped me in lasting ways. I spent 10 years in Wisconsin and 9 years in New York City, and both chapters expanded how I think about community, culture, beauty, and connection.

Food is one of the great loves of my life, and travel feels most meaningful to me when it opens a door into how people really live. I’m always drawn to the places, meals, conversations, and visual details that make a place memorable long after I’ve left it.

I also love meeting new people, reading, writing, and making art. I have a deeply visual brain, and I notice color instinctively enough that in another life I probably could have been a colorist.

A Personal Snapshot

The places that shaped me, the things I love, and the sensibility I bring into every part of life.

What matters most to me is connection: to people, to place, to beauty, to culture, and to the small details that make life feel textured and alive.

A Life Shaped by Curiosity

I’m someone who pays close attention. I love the feeling of discovering a city through its food, understanding a person through conversation, or getting completely pulled into a book, a piece of writing, or a visual world that feels fully realized.

That curiosity shows up everywhere in my life. It is part of why I travel the way I do, why I care so much about atmosphere and detail, and why I’m drawn to thoughtful, interesting people.

Food, Travel, and Discovery

I love food more than anything in the world. It is one of the deepest sources of joy in my life, and one of the ways I feel most connected to culture, memory, and other people.

Travel becomes meaningful to me through the foods I discover along the way. I’m drawn to the market stall someone remembers forever, the local restaurant people return to with loyalty, the bakery that gives a neighborhood its rhythm, and the meal that tells you something true about where you are.

Reading, Writing, Art, and Color

I’m an avid reader and writer, and both are central to how I process the world. I love language, observation, and the feeling that a well-made sentence can suddenly make something clearer, sharper, or more alive.

I’m also a creative artist with a deeply visual mind. Color, tone, and mood register immediately for me, and I notice the subtleties that shape how something feels. That sensitivity is part of how I experience design, beauty, and everyday life.

Places That Shaped Me

The cities and seasons of life I’ve moved through say more about me personally than a conventional biography ever could.

The Beginning

Ann Arbor, Michigan

Ann Arbor is where my story starts. It gave me a strong sense of identity and an early appreciation for ideas, culture, and community. That Midwestern grounding is still a meaningful part of who I am.

A Full Decade

Wisconsin

I spent 10 years in Wisconsin, and it shaped a great deal of my adult life. It was a chapter of independence, personal growth, and long-form friendship, and it still carries a lot of meaning for me.

Nine Years

New York City

I lived in New York City for 9 years, and that chapter expanded me in every direction. It sharpened my perspective, deepened my love of meeting people from all walks of life, and gave me constant access to art, ideas, movement, and some of the best food in the world.

Now

Travel, New Chapters, and Staying Curious

These days, travel continues to shape how I experience the world. I feel most alive when I’m discovering a new place, connecting with new people, and finding the meal I’ll remember long after I leave.

At the center of it all, I care about a life that feels thoughtful, curious, creative, and deeply connected to other people.

What Matters Most to Me

I care about a life that feels thoughtful, warm, curious, and fully awake. I’m drawn to people with depth, to beauty that feels lived-in rather than polished for show, and to experiences that leave a lasting imprint because they were real, generous, and alive.
