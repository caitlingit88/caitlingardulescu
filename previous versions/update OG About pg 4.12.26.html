About

Caitlin Gardulescu

Personally, I am someone who is energized by people, creativity, travel, and great food.

I'm from Ann Arbor, Michigan, and the places I've lived have shaped me in meaningful ways. I spent 10 years in Wisconsin, 9 years in New York City, and now travel often enough that discovering new places and new meals has become one of the great joys of my life.

I love food more than anything in the world, and one of the things that excites me most about travel is the chance to discover and try new foods across the globe. For me, meals are never just about what is on the plate. They are about culture, memory, curiosity, and connection.

I also love meeting new people, reading, writing, and making art. I have a deeply visual brain and such a strong sensitivity to color that I could probably have been a colorist in another life.

Places That Shaped Me

The Beginning

Ann Arbor, Michigan

My hometown

Ann Arbor is where my story starts. It gave me a strong sense of identity, curiosity, and appreciation for culture, ideas, and community.

  • Midwestern roots
  • Early love of creativity, expression, and people
  • A foundation that still feels like home
10 Years

Wisconsin

A full decade of life and growth

I lived in Wisconsin for 10 years, a chapter that shaped a great deal of my adult life. It was a meaningful stretch of personal growth, long-form friendship, and building independence.

  • Ten years of memories, routines, and relationships
  • A formative chapter in who I became
  • A place that still holds a lot of meaning for me
9 Years

New York City

Energy, pace, and endless inspiration

I spent 9 years in NYC, and that chapter expanded me in every direction. It sharpened my perspective, deepened my love of meeting people from all walks of life, and gave me constant access to art, ideas, and some of the best food in the world.

  • Nine years of personal and creative evolution
  • A city that fed my love of people, culture, and movement
  • Countless memorable meals and conversations
Today

Travel, Discovery, and New Chapters

Living globally and staying curious

These days, travel plays a meaningful role in how I experience the world. I feel most alive when I am discovering a new place, connecting with new people, and finding the meal I will remember long after I leave.

  • Food as one of my deepest joys
  • Travel as a path to discovery and connection
  • A life shaped by curiosity, beauty, and experience

Beyond the Bio

Reader & Writer

I am an avid reader and writer, and both have always been central to how I make sense of the world. I love language, observation, and the way good writing can create clarity, intimacy, and feeling.

Creative Practice

I am also a creative artist with a strong visual instinct. Color, composition, and mood stand out to me immediately, and I have the kind of eye for color that makes me think I could technically be a colorist.

What Matters Most

At the center of it all, I care about connection. Whether it is through conversation, travel, a shared meal, a piece of writing, or a work of art, I am most fulfilled when I am learning from people and experiencing the world with openness and depth.

I always enjoy meeting thoughtful, interesting people from different places and backgrounds.
