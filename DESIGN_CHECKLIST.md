# Design Checklist

Use this checklist before merging any design-facing change.

## Brand & Messaging
- [ ] Copy sounds professional, trustworthy, and inviting (not salesy or pushy).
- [ ] Messaging clearly addresses sourcing, operations, vendor partnerships, and assortment strategy.
- [ ] Content is written for SMB teams in food, grocery, and retail.

## Visual Consistency
- [ ] `#2b2b2d` is used for primary typography/accent details and top bar treatment.
- [ ] Gold accents are used intentionally for emphasis and CTAs.
- [ ] Fonts feel contemporary and professional, with strong readability.
- [ ] Spacing and hierarchy remain consistent across sections.

## Interaction & Motion
- [ ] Live elements are subtle, modern, and non-distracting.
- [ ] Hover/focus states are visible and consistent.
- [ ] `prefers-reduced-motion` is respected for decorative animation.

## Contact Section
- [ ] Contact area is visually prominent and easy to scan.
- [ ] Email, phone, and booking CTA are visible without confusion.
- [ ] Contact copy reinforces trust and business outcomes.

## Responsive & Accessibility
- [ ] No horizontal scrolling on mobile or desktop.
- [ ] Tested at `375px`, `640px`, `768px`, and desktop (`>=1200px`).
- [ ] Text/background contrast is readable.
- [ ] Interactive elements are keyboard reachable and clearly labeled.

## Final QA
- [ ] Quick smoke test of key pages and navigation links.
- [ ] Screenshot check completed for desktop and mobile.
- [ ] `git diff` reviewed for unintended style/content regressions.
